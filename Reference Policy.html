Huie Reference Policy

What is Huie Reference?

Huie Reference allows patrons to quickly and conveniently ask questions of librarians and staff. This service is provided for Henderson State University students, faculty, and staff. Students enrolled in Henderson's off-campus or online courses are encouraged to contact the library via email first in order to recieve a more detailed respose. Other users are welcome to use the service for questions that deal specifically with the university or the library and its collections.

Types of assistance provided.

In general, questions in person and over the phone should be shorter, fact-based questions including, but not limited to:

  • Starting points for research and database use
  • Catalog help and resouce locations
  • Campus information and directions

For more in-depth research assistance, an initial consultation with a librarian via email is recommended.

We do NOT answer questions involving:

  • Providing specific answers for class assignments
  • Extended homework help (will make referral to appropriate tutors)
  • Verification of citations or formatting (will make referral to the Writing Center)
  • Requests for legal, medical, financial, or tax advice

Hours

In general, a librarian will be available to answer questions and provide research assistance during weekday library hours. Emails will receive a response within 24 hours.

Privacy

Interactions between patrons and library staff are confidential and will be shared only with other library staff in order to enhance service. Anonymous usage statistics may be gathered and may be used in library reports or publications. Information about specific individuals (including names, IP or email addresses, phone numbers, etc.) will not be retained. An exception to the above will be made in cases of inappropriate or dangerous behavior, such as threatening or harassing library staff or others, in which case information will be shared with library administration and/or law enforcement officials.
