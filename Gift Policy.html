General Guidelines

Huie Library welcomes donations of books and information in other formats that enhance the Library's ability to support the teaching and research missions of Henderson State University.

  • Donors will sign a Deed of Gift.
  • Gifts are accepted with the understanding and agreement that the Library makes all necessary decisions as to the retention, use, or disposal of all materials.
  • A list that includes Title, Author, and Date of publication can be prepared by the donor for the librarians to check for duplicates already in the collection before items are brought to the Library.
  • For the Huie collection, there is interest in new, high quality, scholarly books and resources. Items generally not needed include magazines and journals, newspapers, or anything in poor physical condition.
  • Items directly related to the history of the University and its community may also be of interest.

CONTACT: Please contact Linda Evans, 870-230-5315, or Natalie Scrimshire, 870-230-5271, if you are considering donating materials for the Huie Library collection.

Appraisal and Acknowledgement

Staff of the Huie Library cannot provide appraisal of value of books or other materials. A copy of the Deed of Gift can be provided upon request by the donor.

Monetary Gifts

Huie Library gratefully accepts monetary gifts for use in building the collection or other purposes. Please contact Natalie Scrimshire at 870-230-5271 if you are considering making a monetary gift to the Library.
