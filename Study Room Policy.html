Huie Library provides twelve study rooms to Henderson students. Other patrons may ask at the Circulation Desk for one-time use of a study room.

Reserving Study Rooms

  • Students may make reservations using our online Reserve a Study Room service.
  • Only one reservation is accepted at a time.
  • Reservations are for a maximum of two hours.
  • Loud or disruptive groups may be asked to leave.

First Floor study rooms: Both individual students and groups may reserve first floor study rooms.

Second Floor study rooms: Groups of two or more students have priority use of second floor study rooms. Individual students using the room may be asked to leave if a group needs the room.

Group Project Rooms: The first floor Group Projects Lab, near the Reference Desk, offers a large monitor so that all members of a group may work on a project together. The second floor Group Study Room has room for larger groups.
