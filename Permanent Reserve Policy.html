Items on permanent reserve are available for three-day check out to HSU students, faculty, and staff.

Permanent reserve items may not be renewed and must remain on the shelf at Huie Library for at least twenty-four hours before the patron may recheck the item.

Permanent reserve materials will be available to OBU students, faculty, staff and community patrons for a two-hour, in-library use only.

Permanent reserve items for these patrons can be renewed for an additional two-hour in-library use if no other patron has requested the item. The item must then remain on the shelf at Huie Library for at least twenty-four hours before the patron may recheck the item.
