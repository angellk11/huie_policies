Art Exhibits

Huie Library hosts senior art student exhibitions and, with prior consent, other visiting exhibits. Artists are responsible for mounting and dismantling exhibits. Security is not guaranteed. Exhibits may occasionally contain work that is of an adult nature.

Greene Room Policies

Patrons 18 years of age and older may use the Greene Room.

Study Rooms

Huie Library provides a number of study rooms to Henderson students. Students can reserve these rooms online on our Reserve a Study Room page. For more information on reserving the study rooms, please see our Study Rooms Policy.

Bulletin Board

Flyers, promotional, and informational materials may be posted by students, groups, and other non-library personnel only on the library's bulletin board located on the first floor, near the first bank of computers. These materials must be approved by the Garrison Center Information Desk before posting. Signage posted without stamped approval will be removed.
